**Last updated:** March 13, 2026

This privacy policy explains how jensdufour.be ("the site") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you visit.

## Who runs this site

This site is operated by Jens Du Four, based in Belgium. For any privacy-related questions, you can reach me at [jensdufour@outlook.com](mailto:jensdufour@outlook.com).

## What data is collected

### Server logs

When you visit the site, the hosting provider may automatically collect standard server log data such as your IP address, browser type, referring page, and the pages you visit. This data is used solely for security monitoring and troubleshooting.

### Cookies

This site uses cookies that are strictly necessary for the website to function. No advertising or tracking cookies are placed by this site.

WordPress may set technical cookies (such as session cookies) required for the site to operate correctly. These do not track you across other websites.

### Analytics

This site does not use any third-party analytics services. No personal data is collected for analytics purposes.

### Comments

This site does not have a comment system. No personal data is collected through comments.

### Contact forms

If you contact me via email or LinkedIn, I will only use the information you provide to respond to your inquiry. Your contact details are not shared with third parties or used for marketing.

### Embedded content

Blog posts may embed content from third-party services (such as Sessionize for speaking events). These services may collect data about your visit according to their own privacy policies.

## How your data is used

Any data collected is used exclusively to:

- Ensure the site functions correctly
- Respond to inquiries you send directly
- Monitor and maintain site security

## Data sharing

Your personal data is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties, except where required by law or necessary for hosting the site (e.g., the hosting provider processing server logs).

## Data retention

Server logs are retained according to the hosting provider's standard retention periods. Any personal data from direct communication is kept only as long as necessary to handle your inquiry.

## Your rights under GDPR

As a visitor from the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

- Access the personal data held about you
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Object to or restrict processing of your data
- Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact me at [jensdufour@outlook.com](mailto:jensdufour@outlook.com).

## Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
