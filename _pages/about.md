## Hey, I'm Jens

I'm a Cloud Endpoint Solution Engineer based in Belgium with over four years of experience driving **Intune** adoption and cloud-native **Unified Endpoint Management** across **Entra ID**, **Defender**, **Windows 365**, and **Azure Virtual Desktop**.

At **Microsoft**, I work as a bridge between customers and engineering. I run UATs, synthesize telemetry and support signals, co-create workarounds with product teams, and translate field insights into priorities, documentation changes, and reusable playbooks.

## What I Do

- **UEM and Security**: Intune, Intune Suite, MDM/MAM, Conditional Access, Windows 365, Azure Virtual Desktop, Zero Trust
- **Cross-Cloud Scenarios**: Intune combined with Entra ID and Defender for Endpoint
- **Customer Acceleration**: Scenario design workshops, critical escalations turned into reusable playbooks, preview validation, and change management
- **Prototyping and Automation**: Copilot Studio, PowerShell, Azure Automation and Runbooks
- **Signal Synthesis**: UAT outcomes, telemetry analysis, whitespace analysis, and turning signals into product improvements
