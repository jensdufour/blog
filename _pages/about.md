## Hey, I'm Jens

I'm a Cloud & Security architect from Belgium, passionate about modern workplace management, identity, and endpoint security.

By day I help organizations adopt and secure **Microsoft Intune**, **Entra ID**, **Windows 365**, and the broader **Microsoft 365** ecosystem. By night I write about it here.

- **Identity & Access** — Entra ID, Conditional Access, passwordless authentication
- **Cloud Infrastructure** — Azure, Windows 365, Linux device management
- **Automation** — Logic Apps, PowerShell, Graph API, CI/CD pipelines
